About GGSIPU

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) stands as a beacon of higher education in Delhi, India, fostering innovation and academic excellence since its inception in 1998. With a steadfast commitment to providing quality education, GGSIPU has emerged as a premier institution, offering a diverse array of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. Our university is renowned for its focus on professional education, particularly in technology, management, and medical sciences, preparing students to excel in their chosen fields and contribute meaningfully to society. At GGSIPU, we believe in nurturing not just academic prowess but also holistic development, encouraging students to engage in extracurricular activities, research, and community service. Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and industry-aligned curriculum ensure that students receive a well-rounded education, equipping them with the skills and knowledge to thrive in an ever-evolving global landscape. Join us at GGSIPU, where your future is our vision, and together, we can shape a brighter tomorrow.

MBA Courses

  • MBA (General) -provides a broad overview of various business management areas, equipping students with a foundational understanding of key disciplines like finance, marketing, and operations, rather than specializing in a specific field.
  • MBA (Analytics)-equips students with the skills to analyze data, make data-driven decisions, and solve business problems using analytics, statistics, and computing sciences.
  • MBA (Financial Analytics)-equips graduates with the skills to analyze financial data, make data-driven decisions, and improve business performance, leading to careers in financial analysis, investment management, and more

USMS Rankings and Overview

NIRF India Ranking: 62nd in 2022
Outlook ICare Annual Ranking: 14th in 2023
Times B School Survey: 12th in 2022

University School of Management Studies (USMS), an on-campus B-School of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, is ranked 62nd in the NIRF (2022) under Management category. It has been ranked 14th in the Outlook iCare B-School Rankings 2023 and 12th in Times B-School Rankings 2022 and is amongst the leading public MBA institutions in India.

USMS Mission

To develop young managers with the capability to understand and analyse the emerging business scenario for effective decision-making while maintaining higher domains of consciousness and professional ethics.

Core Values

Innovation, Excellence, Integrity and Transparency
